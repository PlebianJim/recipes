Lamb and Spinich Curry with Whole Spices

Ingredients:

  • WHOLE SPICES (FRY FIRST)
  • 3 tablespoons Ghee or oil
  • 5 black cardamoms
  • 5-10 smoked garlic cloves
  • 4-5 finger chillis
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoons of black mustard seeds
  • 1 teapsoon of black peppercorns
  • MASALA
  • 200-400g lamb cut into small pieces
  • 2-3 tablespoons of tomato puree
  • Bag of spinich
  • 400ml (or a carton) of tomato passata
  • 1 tablespoon of garam masala
  • half a tablespoon of ground cumin
  • half a tablespoon of ground corriandor
  • 2-3 tablespoons of Dunns River lamb seasoning: click
  • 5-6 large tomatoes
  • 1 litre vegetable stock

Method:

  1. Add ghee or oil to the pan and heat until viscous or close to spitting
  2. Add all the whole spices to the pan and keep moving them around for about 3 minutes. Don't let them burn, keep them moving. You are aiming for a strong frgrance to be released
  3. Add the lamb and fry it off in the whole spices
  4. Add all the ground spices and Dunns River lamb seasoning and stir together/ The mixture will get really dry. Stir-fry for about a minute to mix it all together
  5. Add all the tomato ingredients and the stock and stir
  6. Blend the spinich (or leave whole if that's your preference) and add too the pan
  7. Bring to the boil, reduce heat and then reduce the mixture. Alternatively, before you add the tomatoes, move the whole mixture into the slow cooker THEN add all the tomatoes and stock and cook for 4 hours on high or 8 on low
  8. Eat it
